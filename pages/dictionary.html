Acronym Stands For Definition
RZM Reichzeugmeisterei Was responsible for the central supply of all kinds of uniforms, uniform parts and equipment to members of Nazi organizations. A license could be purchased by a manufcaturer, All pieces of equipment had to be labelled with a visible RZM copyright protection symbol and a product-assigned RZM number that contained encoded data about the textile sector, material group, producer number and year of production.
